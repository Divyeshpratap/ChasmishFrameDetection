Find Similar Spectacles that you always dreamt of!

This project aims to find similar spectacles from a comprehensive collection of all spectacles from Lenskart Product Listings.

How It Works:

To find a similar spectacle, please follow the steps below:

  1. Take a picture of the spectacle against a white background (if a photo is not already available), and then upload the picture in JPEG format by clicking on "Choose Image".
  2. Click on the "Upload" button and wait for the process to complete.
  3. The page will automatically redirect to a new page, where the list of 10 most similar spectacles will appear, along with their product IDs.
